Why are we looking for African artists?

Africa is a diverse continent full of fascinating cultures, stunningly beautiful scenery, and extraordinary animals. Africa has wonderfully unique landscapes, tropical beaches, ancient history, geographical wonders and vibrant cities. From the world’s highest sand dunes in the Namib desert to the Victoria Falls in Zambia to the natural landmarks, wildlife reserves and jaw-droppingly beautiful landscapes of Nigeria, Africa offers an immense array of inspirations for any artist in the world. We believe an artist from Africa would be well-suited to draw from those inspirations.
